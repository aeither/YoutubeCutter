FAQ

  • What can I download?

    You can download all public videos available on Youtube. Be sure to only download uncopyrighted content.

  • How do I download my own playlists?

    The playlist privacy settings has to be set to public, otherwise the app won't find it.

  • It isn't working, what should I do?

    Try to close and reopen the app. If you are behind a VPN, try disabling it.
